SubIMDB is the largest structured corpus of subtitles available online.

It includes over 38,000 subtitles extracted with the help of OpenSubtitles and IMDb.

It contains over 150,000,000 words, which is more than three times the size of SUBTLEXus.

It has been used in state-of-the-art solutions to various tasks, such as:
  • Synonym Ranking
  • Lexical Simplification
  • Psycholinguistic Inference
  • Complex Word Identification

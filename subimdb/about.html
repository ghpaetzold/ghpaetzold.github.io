SubIMDB is a structured corpus of subtitles that captures everyday language.

It includes 38,274 subtitles extracted with the help of OpenSubtitles and IMDb.

It contains 228,622,257 words in 39,050,417 lines.

It has been used in state-of-the-art solutions to various tasks, such as:
  • Lexical Simplification
  • Psycholinguistic Inference
  • Complex Word Identification
